Our Journey & Legacy

What began as a single School of Health Sciences in 2012 has grown into a multidisciplinary powerhouse — now comprising six academic schools, cutting-edge research labs, and a thriving innovation hub. NIPAS gained its charter status through unwavering dedication to academic quality, and today we collaborate with over 30 international universities and 100+ industry partners.

Our alumni network of 7,200+ professionals are making impactful contributions across healthcare, artificial intelligence, business leadership, and public policy on five continents. We take pride in fostering an environment where research meets practice, and where every student is empowered to build solutions that matter.
