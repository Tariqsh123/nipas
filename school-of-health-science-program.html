School of Health Sciences

Advancing healthcare through education, research, and clinical innovation.

The School of Health Sciences at NIPAS is dedicated to producing next-generation healthcare leaders, researchers, and practitioners. With state-of-the-art simulation labs, research centers, and partnerships with leading hospitals, we offer an immersive learning environment that bridges theory and practice.

Apply Now

Why NIPAS Health Sciences?

Our programs are designed in collaboration with healthcare industry leaders, ensuring that graduates possess both theoretical depth and practical proficiency. We emphasize evidence-based practice, ethical decision-making, and community health impact.

Students benefit from clinical rotations at top hospitals, access to modern simulation centers, and mentorship from faculty who are active researchers and practitioners. The School also hosts an annual health symposium and offers research grants for innovative projects.
