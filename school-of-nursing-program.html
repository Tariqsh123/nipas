School of Nursing Sciences

Advancing nursing practice through education, research, and clinical excellence.

The School of Nursing Sciences at NIPAS is dedicated to preparing highly skilled nursing professionals, educators, and researchers. With modern nursing skills labs, simulation hospitals, and strong clinical affiliations, we provide a practice-oriented learning environment aligned with global healthcare standards.

Apply Now

Why NIPAS Nursing Sciences?

Our nursing programs are developed in collaboration with leading healthcare institutions to ensure graduates are fully prepared for real-world clinical environments. We focus on patient-centered care, ethical practice, leadership, and evidence-based nursing.

Students gain hands-on experience through clinical rotations in hospitals, community health centers, and specialized care units. Our faculty includes experienced nurses, educators, and researchers committed to advancing the nursing profession.
